SlimScreen AI - Friendly Librarian

Your personal research assistant that provides clear definitions and context for any highlighted text. Get instant insights without leaving your current page!

To use SlimScreen on any webpage, drag this bookmarklet to your bookmarks bar:

SlimScreen: Off

How It Works:

1. Drag the bookmarklet to your bookmarks bar
2. Visit any webpage and click the bookmarklet to activate
3. Highlight any text and press Ctrl+Shift+X
4. Get instant definitions and context from your friendly librarian
5. Ask follow-up questions directly in the widget

Features:

  • Quick Definitions: Instantly get clear explanations for technical terms, jargon, or any text
  • Drag & Resize: Position the widget anywhere on your screen
  • Save Conversations: Export your insights for later reference
  • Works Everywhere: Compatible with most websites
