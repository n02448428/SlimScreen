SlimScreen AI with Lexi

Your personal research assistant with a sleek, modern interface. Lexi helps you understand any text with concise definitions and explanations - directly on your screen without disrupting your flow.

To use SlimScreen on any webpage: Drag this bookmarklet to your bookmarks bar:

Lexi: Research Assistant

How to Use:

1
Drag the bookmarklet to your bookmarks bar
2
Visit any webpage and click the bookmarklet to open Lexi
3
Highlight any text and press Ctrl+Shift+X to get an instant definition
4
Ask follow-up questions directly in the chat interface
5
Use the minimize button (━) to shrink Lexi to a compact bar

Features:

  • Instant Definitions: Highlight text and press Ctrl+Shift+X for clear, concise explanations
  • Sleek Design: Dark mode interface with modern aesthetics and minimal footprint
  • Fully Draggable: Position the widget anywhere on your screen for optimal viewing
  • Minimize Option: Keep Lexi available but out of the way as a compact bar
  • Save Conversations: Export your insights with one click for future reference
  • Page Context: Lexi can summarize the current page to help with your research

Mobile/Tablet Users:

→
iOS (Safari): Add a bookmark, then edit it and paste the bookmarklet code in the URL field
→
Android: Create a new bookmark and paste the bookmarklet code as the URL
