Lexi - Your Research Assistant

Your personal research assistant with a sleek, modern interface. Lexi helps you understand any text with concise definitions and explanations - directly on your screen without disrupting your flow.

To use Lexi on any webpage: Drag this bookmarklet to your bookmarks bar:

Lexi: Research Assistant

How to Use:

1
Drag the bookmarklet to your bookmarks bar
2
Visit any webpage and click the bookmarklet to open Lexi
3
Highlight any text and press Ctrl+Shift+X to get an instant definition
4
Use the minimize button (━) to shrink Lexi to a compact bar
5
Click the bookmarklet again or the X button to close Lexi

Features:

  • Instant Definitions: Highlight text and press Ctrl+Shift+X for explanations
  • Sleek Design: Dark mode interface with minimal footprint
  • Draggable: Position anywhere on your screen
  • Minimize Option: Keep Lexi available as a compact bar
  • Privacy: Conversations reset when closed
